Help & Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I track my shipment?

Enter your tracking number on the homepage and click “Track”. The system will display your shipment status instantly.

My tracking number is not working

Make sure you entered it correctly. If the issue continues, the shipment may not be registered yet. Try again after 1–2 hours.

What does “In Transit” mean?

Your package is on its way between courier facilities.

What does “Out for Delivery” mean?

Your shipment is with a courier and will be delivered shortly.

My shipment is delayed

Delays may occur due to logistics, weather, or customs. Check again later.

How do I contact support?

You can reach support through the WhatsApp button on the website or through the Contact page.

Is my tracking information secure?

Yes. Your information is encrypted and not shared with third parties.
