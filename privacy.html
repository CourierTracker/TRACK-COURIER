Introduction

At Track Courier, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights. If you have questions, contact us at privacy@trackcourier.example (replace with your contact).

We collect only the data you provide to us to use our tracking services (for example: tracking numbers, email address for notifications, and shipment details). We may also collect technical data (IP address, browser user agent, device type) for analytics and security.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide tracking services, send status notifications, respond to support requests, and improve our service. Where required by law or to protect security, we may use information for fraud detection and investigation.

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with service providers who help operate the site (email providers, analytics providers) — only under contract and only as necessary. We will require processors to implement appropriate safeguards.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, preferences, security, and (with your consent) analytics. You may opt in or out of analytics via the cookie banner. Functional cookies required to provide the service are not optional.

Security & Retention

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purpose collected or as required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise rights, contact privacy@trackcourier.example. We will respond within applicable legal timelines.
